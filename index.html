The concept of the game is to show the difficulties that the one faced will promote theirs to growth. Everyone will face their personal challenges. The challenge in our life will move us in a new direction. The challenges that we face that help us grow and make us stronger. In the game, We used a piggy to implied human characters. The obstacles of strollers represent the piggy is a newborn. The baby piggy needs to jump over each stroller to help herself growing up. It is kind of symbolic of a person's growth story.
