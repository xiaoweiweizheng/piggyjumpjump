The concept of the game is to grow up by overcome difficulties. Everyone will face their personal challenges. The challenge in our life will move us in a new direction. The challenges that we face that help us grow and make us stronger.In the game, We used a piggy to imply human. The obstacles of strollers represents that the piggy is a newborn piggy. The baby piggy needs to jump over each stroller in order to grow up. It is kind of symbolic of a person's growth story.
